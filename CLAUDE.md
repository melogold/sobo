# SOBO: Social Media Boards

## Overview

Sobo is a social media boards web application. Users will create boards that they can pin social media posts to. They can then share these boards with others, who can view or collaborate by adding their own posts. The rationale behind Sobo is replace group chats with various social media links.

## Technology

Sobo wilL use [TanStack](https://tanstack.com/start/latest) with TypeScript.
